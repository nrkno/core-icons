Norsk rikskringkasting (https://nrk.no)

@nrk/core-icons has three distinct types of content: Code, logos and icons.

Code refers to all files that are not image assets. Image assets includes file
types such as SVG, PNG and PDF.

Logos refers to image assets that include trademarks, such as NRK. These asset
names are prefixed with `nrk-logo-` or `nrk-super-`.

Icons refers to all image assets other than logos.
